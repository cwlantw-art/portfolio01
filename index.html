About

Chih-Wen Lan is an architect and researcher whose work sits at the intersection of architectural heritage conservation, cultural history, and digital documentation. Her practice spans German–Taiwanese architectural exchange, foreign-related architecture in Taiwan, and GIS-based spatial analysis.

She holds a PhD from Otto-Friedrich-Universität Bamberg and is currently Assistant Professor in the Department of Architecture at China University of Technology, Taipei.
